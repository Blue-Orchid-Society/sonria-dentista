Sonria Dentista Phase 7 analytics and conversion tracking

Purpose
Measure which pages and actions produce patient lead intent before paid ads begin.


What is implemented in code

Google Analytics 4
The site loads GA4 when NEXT_PUBLIC_GA_MEASUREMENT_ID is configured.

Google Ads conversion tracking
The site loads Google Ads through gtag when NEXT_PUBLIC_GOOGLE_ADS_ID is configured.
Form submissions can send a conversion event when NEXT_PUBLIC_GOOGLE_ADS_CONVERSION_LABEL is configured.

Google Search Console
The root layout adds the verification meta tag when NEXT_PUBLIC_GOOGLE_SITE_VERIFICATION is configured.

Click tracking
The site tracks explicit data-track-event links and also infers common conversion clicks from link URLs.


Events tracked

phone_click
Triggered by phone links.

form_submit
Triggered after the contact form or homepage lead form successfully posts to the API.

appointment_click
Triggered by appointment links and hero appointment CTAs.

intake_click
Triggered by new patient intake form links.

map_click
Triggered by Google Maps or share.google links.

service_cta_click
Triggered by links to service pages and service showcase cards.

insurance_cta_click
Triggered by insurance page links and insurance CTAs.

email_click
Triggered by email links.

review_click
Triggered by location review links.


Client details still needed

GA4 measurement ID

Google Ads conversion ID
Example format: AW-XXXXXXXXXX

Google Ads conversion label
Needed if paid ad conversions should be counted from successful form submissions.

Google Search Console verification token
Can be provided as a meta tag token, DNS verification, or HTML file verification. The current implementation supports the meta tag token.

Confirmed production domain
Needed for GA4, Google Ads, and Search Console property setup.

Testing checklist

Open the live site with GA configured.
Open browser dev tools and confirm gtag loads.
Click a phone link and verify phone_click appears in GA4 DebugView.
Submit a test lead form and verify form_submit appears only after success.
Click appointment, intake, map, service, and insurance CTAs.
Confirm Search Console verification succeeds.
Confirm Google Ads conversion appears after a successful test form submission.
