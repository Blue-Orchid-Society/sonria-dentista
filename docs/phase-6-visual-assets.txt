Sonria Dentista visual assets plan

Current temporary asset approach
The site currently uses high-quality Unsplash images as placeholders. These are meant to make the site feel more polished during buildout, not to replace real Sonria photography long-term.

Photo style guidance

Use:
Warm natural lighting
Real smiles
Friendly dentist/team imagery
Clean modern dental office shots
Parent and child moments
Local office details that prove the practice is real

Avoid:
Extreme closeups of dental procedures
Cold hospital-like images
Overly fake stock poses
Generic corporate healthcare imagery
Graphic before/after images without approval


Implementation note
Images are currently stored in JSON fields where possible, so replacing stock later should mostly mean changing URLs in content data instead of rewriting page components.
